VMFive
VMFive is a cloud-based technology company, targeting global mobile market with its leading virtualization and streaming technologies.

Our premiere innovative product, AdPlay, is a cloud service that transforms mobile apps into playable ads, and enable users to try apps before downloading.

All of our members are from top schools and leading companies in Asia, we have good experiences in technology research, product development and business development.
